How it works

Each weight gets a learnable gate:
gate = sigmoid(gate_score) ∈ (0,1)
pruned_weight = weight × gate

Total loss:
L = CrossEntropy + λ × Σ(all gates)

The L1 term gives a constant gradient pushing every gate toward 0. A gate stays open only if the classification benefit outweighs λ. Redundant connections are pruned automatically — no post-training step needed.
